      Neuro physiotherapist

      Neurological rehabilitation frequently improves function, alleviates symptoms, and enhances the patient's well-being. Neurorehabilitation is a multifaceted medical approach that tries to aid in the recovery process following a nervous system damage and to reduce and/or compensate for any functional impairments caused by the injury. It is intended to aid in the treatment of people suffering from nervous system or neurological disorders. Rehabilitation attempts to improve a patient's function, alleviate debilitating symptoms, and enhance their overall quality of life.

      Deconditioning

      Deconditioning is a term that refers to reversible changes in the body caused by a reduction in physical activity as a result of extended hospitalisation or other bedridden situations. Its severity is determined by a variety of factors, including the patient's age, the period of physical inactivity, and any coexisting disorders. We facilitate deconditioning rehabilitation services in a manner that is beneficial to the patients. The prescribed gradual training programme improves muscle strength, mobility, ADL performance, walking and endurance as well as balance and coordination.

      Orthopedic physiotherapist

      Orthopedic physiotherapy is a significant discipline of medicine that focuses on the treatment of skeletal system injuries and disorders, as well as associated muscles, joints, and ligaments. Additionally, it encompasses pre- and post-operative hip, shoulder, and knee therapy. Our home physiotherapist will begin by doing a thorough clinical assessment and examination, followed by the administration of appropriate treatments. We are committed to identifying the underlying source of the problem and then resolving it. The orthopaedic physiotherapist's treatment goal is to alleviate pain, increase joint range of motion, improve strength and flexibility, and restore the patient to full function.
