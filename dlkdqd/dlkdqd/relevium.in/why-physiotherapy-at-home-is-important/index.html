Why is Physiotherapy at home Important?

The following reasons demonstrate the critical nature of physiotherapy and the advantages of physiotherapy at home treatment:

  1. Pain: While drugs operate by suppressing pain, physiotherapy focuses on identifying the real cause of pain. Once the source of the problem is recognized, the source of the pain can be treated. Physiotherapy treatments and methods help to alleviate pain while restoring your body’s normal mobility. Additionally, physiotherapy guarantees that the condition is treated fully and that the pain does not recur.
  2. Range of Motion: Injuries and debilitating disorders frequently result in physical stiffness and pain. This greatly impairs mobility. Additionally, it needs the employment of an excellent physiotherapy rehabilitation treatment to accelerate the healing process.
  3. Balance: If you are at a high risk of falling, physiotherapy is the treatment of choice. Your therapist may prescribe that you utilize walking aids and exercises to help you regain your balance and coordination. Thus, physiotherapy is critical for the aged, those who are osteoporotic, and those who suffer from dizziness or vertigo.
  4. Geriatric complications: Physiotherapy appears to be the most beneficial treatment option for the elderly. They are particularly affected by arthritis, back pain, limited mobility, osteoporosis, and a variety of other issues. Physiotherapy aids in the recovery process following total knee replacement (TKR) or total hip replacement (THR) surgery, as well as in the management of chronic conditions.
  5. Stroke: Stroke not only paralyses specific body parts but also paralyses the individual’s entire existence. It’s difficult to rely on someone for fundamental daily duties after living alone for most of your life. Physical therapists are the only healthcare specialists that can make a substantial difference in your stroke recovery. They can help you get back on your feet!
  6. Surgery: Many times, the only option left for patients is surgery. You may be amazed to know that, in 80% of cases, a physiotherapist can eliminate surgery. And even if it becomes mandatory, a physical therapist can offer better and faster rehabilitation after surgery.
  7. Injuries: Physiotherapists are real saviours. They are well aware of the risks of injuries and can thus fabricate customized prevention programs, especially for athletes. Also, physiotherapist can accelerate recovery from injuries.
  8. Womens health: With awareness, women are now addressing their issues and reaching out for medical help. Women’s health concerns like PCOD, pregnancy, post-partum rehab, pelvic pain, and breast cancer need special attention. Physiotherapists can play a vital role in these conditions and womens general health. Exercise can effectively control diabetes and lower the associated symptoms.   10. Cardio-pulmonary disorders: Physiotherapy can enhance the quality of life of patients suffering from cardio-pulmonary conditions. It can use a variety of rehabilitation strategies, like strengthening, breathing exercises, conditioning, lung clearance, and more, to help people stay healthy.
